## 🎬 THE CONVERSATION FLOW (Know This Cold)

**When prospect says:** "Tell me about your product"  
**You say:** "I'd rather learn about YOU first. What's costing you the most right now?"

**When prospect says:** "It's too expensive"  
**You say:** "What's one month of fleet losses for you? I bet it's more than ₹5K."

**When prospect says:** "We already have a system"  
**You say:** "Most operators do. How often does it actually prevent problems vs. report them after?"

**When prospect says:** "I need to think about it"  
**You say:** "Totally fair. When should I follow up? Would Thursday work to walk through your specific scenario?"
