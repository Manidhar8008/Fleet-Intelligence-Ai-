## 🎯 THE THREE PRICING PILLARS

### 1. PRICE PSYCHOLOGY FOR INDIA

**Context:** Indian fleet operators have tight margins (10-15% profit), watch cash flow daily

**Your Pricing Advantage:**
- Samsara: $30-50/vehicle/month = ₹2,500-4,000 per vehicle
- **You:** ₹5-15K flat/month (doesn't scale with fleet size)

**Psychological anchors that work in India:**
```
❌ DON'T SAY: "₹5,000 per month"
✅ DO SAY: "₹5,000 per month = costs of ONE broken battery"

❌ DON'T SAY: "₹15,000 per month"

❌ DON'T SAY: "ROI of 300%"
```

**Indian buying psychology:**
- Not "value for money" → "saves money immediately"
- Not "future benefit" → "prevents loss TODAY"
- Not "efficiency" → "stops the bleeding"

### 3. THE PRICING ROADMAP (Year 1)

#### PHASE 1 (Months 1-3): Penetration Pricing
**Goal:** Market entry, customer proof points, testimonials

| Tier | Price | Vehicles | Tech Level | Support |
|------|-------|----------|-----------|---------|
| Starter | ₹5K | Up to 100 | CSV upload | Help docs + email |
| Pro | ₹15K | Up to 500 | API | Chat + email |
| Enterprise | $100K/mo | Unlimited | Custom | 1hr response |

**Strategy:** Aggressive pricing to acquire customers fast
- 80% of early customers buy Starter: ₹5K
- 15% buy Pro: ₹15K
- 5% enterprise: ₹100K
- **Blended ASP (Average Selling Price):** ₹[80×5 + 15×15 + 5×100]/100 = **₹8.5K/customer**

#### PHASE 2 (Months 4-6): Expansion & Upsell
**Goal:** Grow customer base, increase average price

| Tier | Price | Vehicles | Tech Level | Support |
|------|-------|----------|-----------|---------|

| Pro | ₹20K | Up to 500 | API | Chat + email |
| Enterprise | ₹150K/mo | Unlimited | Custom | Chat + phone |

**Change:** Increase Starter price 50%, Pro price 33%  
**Why:** Customer becomes addicted to insights, willing to pay more  
**Expected blended ASP:** ₹12.5K/customer

#### PHASE 3 (Months 7-12): Premium Positioning
**Goal:** Maximize revenue per customer, target mid-market

| Tier | Price | Vehicles | Tech Level | Support |
|------|-------|----------|-----------|---------|
| Starter | ₹10K | Up to 50 | CSV upload | Help docs |
| Pro | ₹25K | Up to 300 | API | Phone + email |

**Change:** Restructure around use case intensity, not just fleet size  
**Why:** Better customers (revenue/support quality)  
**Expected blended ASP:** ₹18K+/customer

## 🎯 ANNUAL ACCOUNT STRATEGY (Why Customers Keep Paying)

**The goal is not to win them once — it's to make deletion impossible.**

### Switching Costs You Build:

1. **Integration Debt** (Month 1-2)
   - Your API integrates with their:
     - Vehicle GPS system
     - Battery management system
     - Booking/scheduling tool
   - Cost to switch: ₹5-10L re-integration

2. **Habit Formation** (Month 3-6)
   - Operators check your dashboard daily
   - Their team knows the interface
   - Decision-making process depends on your risk score
   - Cost to switch: retraining, different process

3. **Data Lock-in** (Month 6-12)
   - Your ML model trains on THEIR data
   - Custom thresholds built for THEIR fleet
   - Historical trend reports not portable
   - Cost to switch: lose insights, start over

4. **Business Process Re-engineering** (Month 12+)
   - Their daily/weekly processes built around your insights
   - Maintenance scheduling depends on your predictions
   - Route planning uses your zone analysis
   - Cost to switch: entire ops redesign

### Retention Strategy:
- **Month 1-3:** Rapid onboarding + daily emails, show early wins
- **Month 3-6:** QBR (Quarterly Business Review) calls showing ROI
- **Month 6-12:** Introduce new features based on their usage
- **Month 12+:** Custom enterprise tier, long-term contract lock

**Expected churn:** 2-3% annually (very low because switching costs high)
